CumaPro Privacy Policy

Published by Mezsondra LTD

Effective date: March 14, 2026

Mezsondra LTD respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data in the CumaPro application.

1. Introduction

By using CumaPro, you consent to the practices described here. Our philosophy is simple: your data stays on your device whenever possible.

2. Data Collection

CumaPro itself does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data.

  • Local usage: App content and settings are stored on your device.
  • No app accounts: You do not need to create an account to use CumaPro.
  • No CumaPro servers: Mezsondra LTD does not upload your personal app content to company-owned servers.

3. Permissions

CumaPro may request limited device permissions only when needed for specific features (for example, media or storage access based on your Android version).

4. Advertising & Third-Party Services

CumaPro includes Google AdMob ads. While the app itself does not collect your personal data, AdMob may process certain identifiers and ad-interaction data to deliver and measure ads.

  • Advertising ID (AAID)
  • Ad usage and interaction metrics
  • Cookies or similar technologies used by Google ad services

You can manage ad personalization from your Google Ad Settings: adssettings.google.com.

5. Children’s Privacy

CumaPro is safe for general audiences, including families, but it is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. Security

We use reasonable measures to keep the app secure. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights related to personal data processed by third-party services such as AdMob.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: contact@mezsondra.com

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
